From “outline my paper” to a measurement study with a shared vocabulary

Scientific writing is the failure mode that hurts most — agents are fluent liars. We outline a measurement study on sustainability in data center networks, four times. Each step adds a skill and removes one failure mode. Click an exercise for the detail.

Common pitfalls
  • Agents invent datasets and testbeds — no /grill-me grounding in what you can actually measure.
  • Agents conflate metrics — no glossary to keep “energy efficiency” and “power consumption” distinct.
  • Agents smooth over methodology gaps — no ADR for what was not measured and why.
