What this is, and what it isn't

✓ This is

A snapshot of how agentic coding tools work in 2026: the vocabulary, the mental models, and a hands-on tour of opencode + Matt Pocock's skill collection. The goal is to make you dangerous enough to decide for yourself what's worth your time.

✗ This is not

A vibe-coding tutorial. A "use ChatGPT for everything" pitch. A survey of every agentic tool on the market. A model-finetuning lecture. We are explicitly skipping those.
